About

A youth-led and youth-centred initiative focusing on the support & empowerment of Vulnerable and needy Christians with an emphasis on employment and education. Buhehe Integrated Livelihood Project helps Vulnerable and needy Christians aged 15 and above who are in/out of school, unemployed or underemployed overcome the barriers to employment through strategic partnerships, workshops and campaigns. .

We seek to dispel the negative stereotypes of vulnerable and needy Christians by giving them a platform to express themselves and the issues affecting their day-to-day lives. Hire the Youth is developed, run and managed by vulnerable and needy Christians because it’s foundation is built on vulnerable and needy Christians to be independent, happy, flourish and work out who they are, irrespective of their background. .

Our Vision

An empowered, enlightened, united Anglican community free from poverty with productive and entrepreneurship skill s for sustainable Development.

Our Mission

To unite, enlighten and empower, the vulnerable and needy Christians to engage in and benefit from sustainable Development.

Action

We work purposefully and with a sustained effort to achieve a long-term impact of solving unemployment so that all vulnerable and needy Christians can have a fair chance at employment..

Collaboration

We recognize that no single person can solve unemployment and its complex effects, therefore we believe that people working together can create greater impact than any one individual or organization can accomplish alone.

Transformation

We believe in constantly learning and taking bold actions that create lasting, systemic change within the society.

Our Objectives
  • • To provide knowledge to marginalized community to plan and manage income generating activities.
  • • To Network with other NGOS in the campaign against HIV/ AIDS
  • • To initate development projects aimed at generating income for the organization.
  • • To solicit for grants, donations, from government, well wishers and NGOs both local and international for the purpose of aiding the marginalized and vulnerable persons.
  • • Equipping famers/Christians with modern farming skills and farming as a business (FaaB)
Why we do what we do
  • 8.2 million Ugandans are 15-30 years of age.
  • 4.5 million Ugandan youth are unemployed.
  • 32.9% of the country population is aged between 10 – 24 years.
  • Our population is growing and more than 77 percent of Ugandans are young people aged 30 or below have unrestricted access to economic opportunities and affordable business training and where they can register impactful participation in decision-making.

Against these key points, Buhehe Integrated Livelihood project's work and efforts are timely to make a generous contribution to ensuring that every young person can access the accurate information to aid and direct them in taking that crucial life decision regarding their live skills and development, a chance at free or fair employment and education.

Our Services

Buhehe Integrated livelihood project is a bridging project that deals in linking the unemployed youth to various opportunities by starting up small scale projects and involving them into such projects.

Piggerry

Buhehe Integrated livelihood project purchases pigglets from all sources and gives them to the members in the club to rear and produce more pigs which are further sold and generates income for the youth in the club

Goat Rearing

Buhehe Integrated livelihood project is one of the leading goat rearing projects in the eastern uganda. Goat rearing was initiated by young peolple in 2017 and later sponsored by the Church of Uganda Buhehe

Poultry

Buhehe Integrated Livelihood project also deals in poulrty rearing both local and cross breed/ high breed poultry which are rared on both free range system and advanced systems respectively

Church Section

Youth livellyhood project operates under church. This project promotes christianity to the members by encouraging them to join and preaching the gospel to them church

Computer Training Hub

youthlivelyhood has bought computers for training the youth of the community and its office work

Tree Planting

Buhehe Integrated Livelhood Project has different tree plantations within the subcounty
